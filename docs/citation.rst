The ESO SupJup Survey — IV. Unveiling the carbon isotope ratio of GQ Lup B and its host star.

Spline forward modeling
-----------------------

``SplineModel`` and the weighted linear fitting approach are adapted from the
**breads** framework. The spline-based continuum model follows Ruffio et al. (2023):

Ruffio et al. (2023), *The Astronomical Journal*, 165, 113,
`doi:10.3847/1538-3881/acb34a <https://doi.org/10.3847/1538-3881/acb34a>`_

Detecting Exomoons from Radial Velocity Measurements of Self-luminous Planets:
Application to Observations of HR 7672 B and Future Prospects.

See also the analytical marginalization formalism in Ruffio et al. (2019, AJ, 158, 200)
and the **breads** code: https://github.com/jruffio/breads
